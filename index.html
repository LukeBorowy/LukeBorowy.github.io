About Me

I'm a student at the University of St. Thomas, and I am currently in my fourth year here. I am majoring in Computer Science, as well as in Spanish. I was previously only minoring in Spanish, but I have decided to switch to a double major instead.

I would consider computer science and programming to be my primary interest, having programmed since childhood in several different languages. I have experienced and enjoyed coding in a wide array of applications, including video games, simulations, machine learning, web development, and robotics. I love working with technical systems and the logical problem-solving is very satisfying when making my own projects.

I decided to major in Spanish just because I enjoyed learning the language. I am following the lingustics track within the major, which interests me due to the similarities to computer science in many aspects, due to the analytical way in which language is studied.

After college, I hope to have a career as a software developer, although I'm not set on a specific subfield yet.
